个人简介 Short Bio

小生谢佳龙，目前正在山东大学控制科学与工程学院攻读博士学位, 研究方向语言引导的目标分割与抓取.
I am Jialong Xie, currently pursuing a PhD degree at the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University, and majoring in language-conditioned object segmentation and grasping.

在生活中，我喜欢户外运动，目前对骑行（土坡车）、徒步、露营、养鱼感兴趣，喜欢通过相机记录自己生活的点点滴滴，希望抓住最后这一点快乐的读书时光和为数不多的青春，多尝试不一样的东西。
In life, I enjoy outdoor activities and am currently interested in cycling (dirt jumping), hiking, camping, and fishkeeping. I like to capture the little moments of my life through my camera, hoping to seize these last joyful moments of my student days and the fleeting youth to try as many different things as possible.
